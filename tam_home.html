Active Ingredients

  1. 1,8-cineole.
  2. α-thujone.
  3. camphor.
  4. α-pinene and borneol.
  5. antimicrobial properties.
  6. antioxidative properties.
  7. terpenoids of the eudesmadien type.
  8. terpenoids of the germacratien type.
  9. coumarins.
  10. acetylenes.

Directions

  1. Add 1 tbl. spoon of tea leaves into a 1L teapot.
  2. pour in hot water, and steep for 3-5 minutes depending on required strength.
  3. pour it into your teacup through a mesh kitchen strainer.
  4. Add honey or sugar to taste.
  5. Consume it within three to four weeks.
  6. You must be careful not to overuse wormwood.

It cured my headaches and fever as well as restoring my lost appetite during Covid19, a must have in your garden.
